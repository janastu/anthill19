We're getting ready for Anthillhacks 2023 - 2024!

Join us this December at Devarayanadurga, Tumkur, Karnataka, India

From the December 25th to January 4th

Get set for the 8th installment of Anthillhacks! Finish off your year camping, cooking and hacking together at Devarayanadurga.

Anthillhacks has always been a collaborative space where anyone is welcome to propose a session.

What you can expect

  1. Build days and feminist technology in community networks
  2. Theories of Change workshops on environmental sustainability and care
  3. Cooking camps in villages
  4. Tech-stack for including low-literates
  5. Learn permaculture and organic farming techniques
  6. Performative arts, crafts and stories everyday

Tentative track ideas and tasks

dWeb experiments, Community Networks, Gender and Environments, Supporting ecosystems of care
